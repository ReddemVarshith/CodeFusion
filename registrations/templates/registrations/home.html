Frequently Asked Questions

What is the registration fee?

The participation fee is ₹399 per head, which covers your entry, utilities, and refreshments.

Who can participate?

Fresher's CodeStorm 2k26 is exclusively for 1st-year students from any recognized college. Team size must be between 3 to 5 members.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptops, chargers, and any hardware components you might need for your project. We'll provide the rest!
